THE PROBLEM

Physical work is invisible.

When you ship software, you push a commit. When you sell services, you log hours. When you nail a board, take an order, or prep a meal, there is no record. No proof. No price tag.

78 million Americans do work like this every day, and the only artifact of their effort is a manager's memory. That's how disputes start. That's why tip pools are unfair. That's why contractors lose hours every week explaining what got done.

The tech to fix it didn't exist 18 months ago. It does now.

CONTRACTORS
Lose hours every week disputing what got done. AIA G702/G703 forms are still filled out by hand.
RESTAURANTS
Owners cannot tell who actually worked the rush versus who stood around. Tip pools and reviews suffer.
EMPLOYERS
Pay for hours, not output. No way to verify physical work without expensive site supervisors.
THE PRODUCT

A clip on camera. A worker app. A manager dashboard.

A proprietary camera module pairs with the worker's phone over Bluetooth. The phone uploads chunks to AI. The manager sees what got done.

01
CAMERA CAPTURES
Lightweight clip on camera mounts to a hard hat, apron, or lapel. Built for 8 hour shifts in tough conditions.
02
PHONE UPLOADS
Bluetooth syncs video to the worker's phone. The phone uploads chunks to AI on a schedule, even on a job site.
03
MANAGER SEES VALUE
Tasks done, time spent, quality grades, and dollar value generated all surface in a clean web dashboard.
